Welcome to to our art club!

We provide classes for art beginners, making us your perfect new adult hobby!

Our art teachers have diverse art techniques and unique experiences when it comes to expressing their creativity through art!

Our daily sessions provide different types of painting tools and painting techniques that our art beginners can participate in.

The types of painting offered in our classrooms are :
history painting - ancient paintings that depict a classical greek, or romanian eighteenth century portraits

portrait painting - uses the photographic characteristics of people's printed snapshots, and takes on a
hand-painted interpretation and sketch

genre painting - these classic paintings take place in century-old settings, and depict day-to-day scenes with distinctive
surroundings and different environments

landscape painting - is natural scenery captured in a painting

still life painting - observes domestic objects in their natural state

Our art teachers provide our classes with amateur art handbooks and tips on how to improve on your painting techniques!
